Pokemon Gold

"Pokemon Gold" takes players on a nostalgic journey through the Johto region, where they embark on a quest to become the ultimate Pokemon Trainer. Set years after the events of the original Pokemon games, Gold introduces new features like day and night cycles, breeding, and the ability to explore both Johto and the neighboring Kanto region. With iconic Pokemon to catch, challenging gyms to conquer, and a sinister new villainous team to thwart, "Pokemon Gold" offers a timeless adventure filled with excitement and discovery.
