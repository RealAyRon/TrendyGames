Resident Evil Village

"Resident Evil Village" plunges players into a nightmarish world of horror and suspense, set in a remote European village shrouded in mystery and terror. Continuing the story of Ethan Winters from "Resident Evil 7: Biohazard," players must navigate through the village's twisted streets and dilapidated buildings while uncovering dark secrets and facing off against unimaginable horrors. As Ethan, players must fight for survival against grotesque creatures, solve intricate puzzles, and uncover the truth behind the enigmatic figure known as Mother Miranda and her twisted experiments. With its stunning visuals, immersive atmosphere, and heart-pounding gameplay, "Resident Evil Village" delivers a chilling and unforgettable experience that will keep players on the edge of their seats until the very end.
